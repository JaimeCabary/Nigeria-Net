🎉 Tech Conference Schedule 🎉

Date: 1st to 4th December, 2024.

Location: Senate Building FUTO

🗓️ Schedule of Events

  • 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Registration & Welcome Reception
    • 🎟️ Check in, receive badges, and enjoy light refreshments.
    • 🤝 Networking opportunities with fellow participants.
  • 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Opening Ceremony
    • 👋 Welcome speech by the conference organizer.
    • 📅 Overview of the day's schedule and activities.
  • 11:00 AM - 11:45 AM: Keynote Address
    • 💡 Topic: The Future of Technology
    • 👤 Speaker: Joseph Azuwike
  • 11:45 AM - 12:30 PM: Introduction to Cryptocurrency
    • 🔍 Session Type: Workshop
    • Description: Overview of cryptocurrencies and their impact on the economy.
    • 👤 Speaker: Nkechi Okoro Carrol
  • 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch Break & Networking 🍽️
    • 🍴 Enjoy a catered lunch while networking with peers and speakers.
  • 1:30 PM - 3:30 PM: Hackathon Kickoff 🚀
    • Description: Teams will brainstorm and start coding projects for the competition.
    • 👤 Speaker: Demi Leigh Tebow
  • 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Talent Show & Activities 🎤
    • 🎶 Participants showcase their talents (music, dance, etc.).
    • 🎉 Activity: Fun group games and challenges.
  • 4:00 PM - 4:30 PM: Closing Ceremony & Prize Distribution 🏆
    • 🎊 Announcement of hackathon winners and distribution of prizes.
    • 👥 Closing remarks from the organizers.

🎈 Additional Activities

Details of any additional activities will be provided during the conference.
